## Turn On Machine
- Flip the dial switch on the underside of the touchscreen panel FIRST
- Wait for the Shima Seiki Logo to appear, then flip the smaller switch to the on position.
- The machine will begin to make a series of clicks and motions. Once that has ceased, perform a zeroing out or 'tare.' 

### Zeroing Out
- On the button pad, select the pink button labeled "PREPA"
- Select the fourth box from right, which displays a green back arrow and the number 0 at the bottom of an X and Y axis
- The machine will once more make a series of clicks. Once done, move onto the warmup procedure.

## Warm Up Procedure
### Needle Training
- From the home page, select the settings icon (a wrench and screw driver)
- Needle training
- The parameters should be pre-set to 0.60m/s at 2M
- Hit 'START', then press the green drive button on the face of the machine.
- Allow training to complete, then perform a Debug.

### Debug
- From the home page, select the settings icon (a wrench and screw driver)
- Debug Mode
- Press the 'play' icon, then press F5 on the button pad
